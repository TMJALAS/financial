Frequently Asked Questions

What should I prepare for my consultation?

Come prepared with information about your current financial situation, including income, expenses, debts, and savings. Also think about your short-term and long-term financial goals.

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes! The 30-minute discovery call is completely free with no obligation. It's an opportunity for us to understand your needs and for you to see if our services are the right fit.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

We offer both in-person and virtual consultations via video call. Choose whichever format is most convenient for you.

How do I pay for services?

We accept payments via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or cash. Payment details will be provided after your initial consultation when we discuss the service package that best fits your needs.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, we understand that schedules change. Please give us at least 24 hours notice if you need to reschedule your appointment. You can do this through Calendly or by contacting us directly.
